About

My Self-Love Supply's mission is to create and share positivity, self-love and kindness within our community. This project started out in May of 2019 with the goal to inspire and encourage healthy living and self-care, on both a physical and mental level. We are continuously encouraged and motivated by our growing community and committed to creating a safe space online for everyone to inspire and get inspired. Without any further ado, go read our blog or check out our gratitude journal. We poured our hearts and souls into making this journal and we sincerely hope you enjoy it. We welcome any feedback, suggestions and thoughts. Please don't hesitate to give us a message. To get in touch you can DM us on Instagram at My Self-Love Supply or fill out our contact us form.
